By Ryan Larkin - 1968 An artist's observation of the way people walk. Ryan Larkin employs a variety of techniques--line drawing, color wash, etc.--to catch and reproduce the motion of people afoot. The springing gait of youth, the mincing step of the high-heeled female, the doddering amble of the elderly--all are registered with humor and individuality, to the accompaniment of special sound.
